From the picnic-area parking off TN-154, the well-signed loop runs through open hardwood forest to a dramatic sandstone arch spanning 86 feet and standing about 35 feet high. The grade is gentle and the distance short, making it an ideal family hike in this remote CCC park near Big South Fork.
