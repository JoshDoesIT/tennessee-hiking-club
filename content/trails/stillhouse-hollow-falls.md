The trail descends through hardwood forest over rocks, roots, stairs, and small bridges, crossing a shallow stream to reach the falls about two-thirds of a mile in. An unnamed tributary forms small cascades before plunging roughly 75 feet over the bluff into a pool popular as a swimming hole. The return is the steep climb back out.
