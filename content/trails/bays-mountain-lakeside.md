This is the park's signature walk, circling the reservoir on a well-kept path with several footbridges, including a floating bridge over an arm of the lake. Expect rolling but modest climbs and frequent wildlife, beaver dams and lodges, waterfowl, and a lily-pad pond. It stays busy on weekends and connects to the larger Bays Mountain trail system.
