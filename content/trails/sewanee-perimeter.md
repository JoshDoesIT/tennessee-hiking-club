The Perimeter Trail is a 20-mile loop tracing the Cumberland Plateau bluff line around the University of the South. This popular short segment runs from the Memorial Cross out to a string of bluff-edge overlooks where sandstone ledges drop away into the coves below. Keep kids and dogs close to the trail along the open clifftops.
