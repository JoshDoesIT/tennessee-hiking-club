Half the loop runs flat along the Clinch River with views of the dam and its aerating weirs, while the other half climbs to follow a ridge crest on an old road grade left from the 1930s dam construction. Spring brings strong wildflower displays of bloodroot and trout lilies, concentrated near the river. It is a quiet, forested loop a short drive from Knoxville.
