From the connector across from the visitor center, the loop climbs through hardwoods on the High Ridge Trail, then drops to the water and follows the Shoreline Trail along the Bledsoe Creek embayment of Old Hickory Lake. It passes a boat ramp, campground, and picnic areas, and the lakeside woods are a well-known spot for birding and wildlife.
