Opened in 2016 on a repurposed roadway of the historic Harahan rail bridge, Big River Crossing carries walkers and cyclists on a fenced steel-grate deck high above the Mississippi. The paved approach at Martyrs Park climbs gently onto the span, with open views of the Memphis skyline, the Hernando de Soto bridge, and barge traffic below. It is fully accessible and lit nightly; sunrise and sunset crossings are the best.
