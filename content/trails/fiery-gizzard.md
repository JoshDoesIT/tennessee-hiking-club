From the Grundy Forest trailhead the route follows cascading creeks past Blue Hole and Sycamore Falls before climbing onto the Cumberland Plateau rim. A short spur near the turnaround reaches Raven Point, a rocky promontory with a wide view over the gorge. Often ranked among the best hikes in the state, it is long and rugged the whole way.
