The Highland Rim Nature Trail descends from the bluff near the visitor center through wildflower-rich hardwood forest to the water's edge on the Caney Fork arm of Center Hill Lake. It is only about a mile and a half, but loses and regains over 300 feet. The trailhead sits near the park's tall lookout tower, a good orientation stop before or after.
