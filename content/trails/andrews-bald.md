Andrews Bald is the highest grassy bald in the park, an open mountaintop meadow surrounded by spruce-fir forest with sweeping views toward Fontana Lake and the southern mountains. The Forney Ridge Trail leaves the Clingmans Dome parking area and drops through a high-elevation forest of moss, fern, and weathered spruce, with a rocky, root-laced tread that earns the moderate rating on the climb back out. In June the bald blazes with flame azalea and rhododendron; late summer brings blueberries. Pack a layer, since it is cool and often cloudy at 5,800 feet. A parking tag is required park-wide.
