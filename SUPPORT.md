# Support

Need help or have a question? Here's where to go:

- **Found a bug?** Open a
  [bug report](https://github.com/JoshDoesIT/tennessee-hiking-club/issues/new?template=bug_report.yml).
- **Have an idea?** Open a
  [feature request](https://github.com/JoshDoesIT/tennessee-hiking-club/issues/new?template=feature_request.yml).
- **Know a trail we're missing?** Submit a
  [new trail](https://github.com/JoshDoesIT/tennessee-hiking-club/issues/new?template=new_trail.yml).
- **Want to contribute code?** Read [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md).
- **Security issue?** Follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Do not open a public
  issue.

This is a volunteer, open-source project, so responses are best-effort. Please
be patient and kind.
